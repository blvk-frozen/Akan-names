What is the criteria for issuing an Akan Name?

The criteria used is that there are specific names given for each day of the week and they are different according to gender as below:


For Male:

Sunday: Kwasi

Monday: Kwadwo

Tuesday: Kwabena

Wednesday: Kwaku

Thursday: Yaw

Friday: Kofi

Saturday: Kwame


For Female:


Sunday: Akosua

Monday: Adwoa

Tuesday: Abenaa

Wednesday: Akua

Thursday: Yaa

Friday: Afua

Saturday: Ama
